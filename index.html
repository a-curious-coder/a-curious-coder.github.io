Rubik's Cube Solver

For my dissertation, I created a Rubik's Cube Solver using Processing-Java. This involved visualizing the cube in 2D and 3D formats. I developed and applied various search algorithms with some clever heuristics and generated graphs based on the solving results. My Rubik's Cube Solver can solve the traditional and pocket cube in 5 different ways. My next goal is to cater the solver for larger cube sizes, using the 'Reduction Method'.

Invoice Generator

Eager to apply skills I had learned during my degree, I sought after small local businesses offering my services free of charge as I was only after experience. I encountered 'Kelland Dairy', a local milk delivery who outlined their business procedures and they mentioned having to manually write out invoices for each of their customers every fortnight.

I took this opportunity to suggest then develop an invoice generator. This makes use of an online spreadsheet, accessible via google sheets, which is read into my program and has its data preprocessed and validated; this takes mere moments. Next, all that is needed is to click 'Generate' and it sends the data to an invoice-generator API and downloads the resulting pdfs. These are merged into a single file, ready for printing, saving hours of time.
